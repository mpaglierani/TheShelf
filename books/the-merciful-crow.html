GOODREADS REVIEW

A Spellbinding Debut

"The Merciful Crow" by Margaret Owen is an exhilarating fantasy that soars above the rest. Owen's debut novel is a thrilling journey through a world of magic, political intrigue, and a heroine's fight against injustice.

From the opening pages, Owen's narrative grabs hold and doesn't let go. The world-building is spectacular, immersing readers in a richly crafted universe where the caste system is as perilous as the magical threats that loom. The attention to detail in constructing this intricate realm demonstrates Owen's mastery of storytelling.

"The Merciful Crow" stands out for its refreshing take on fantasy tropes. Owen injects new life into the genre with a narrative that combines fast-paced action with thought-provoking themes of power, prejudice, and resilience. The magical system, centered around the unique abilities of each caste, adds an extra layer of intrigue to the unfolding tale.
