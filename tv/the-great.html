A Hilarious Triumph of Historical Satire

The Great emerges as a delightful and irreverent gem in the realm of historical satire. This Hulu original, led by the dynamic performances of Elle Fanning and Nicholas Hoult, takes viewers on a wildly entertaining journey through the eccentric and scandalous court of Catherine the Great.

Elle Fanning's portrayal of Catherine is a revelation—her comedic timing and charismatic presence breathe fresh life into this historical figure, transforming her into a thoroughly modern and relatable heroine. Opposite her, Nicholas Hoult's Peter III is a scene-stealing tour de force, delivering laughs and occasional menace with equal brilliance.

The writing is sharp and witty, with a keen sense of satire that skewers the conventions of period dramas. The dialogue crackles with energy, and the narrative twists and turns keep audiences guessing and laughing in equal measure. The show's ability to balance comedy with moments of genuine emotion is a testament to its narrative prowess.
