LETTERBOXD REVIEW

A Not-so Scary Movie with Deeprooted Lore

At Freddy Fazbear' Pizzeria, the animatronics come to life after dark, and what happens when one sleep deprived security guard falls alseep on the job? From caring for his sister and holding fircely onto memories of the past, Mike takes a new job at a shut down Pizzeria, simply instructed not to let vandals in and do not fall asleep.

The game Five Nights at Freddy's boasts much scarier animatronics in comparison to the movie. These animatronics are painted in a different light, especially once finding out thaty the souls of murdfered children inhabit these beings. They are are shown building a fort and even tickling Mike's younger sister when she comes to stay with him. These difference's have made some fans angry because they go against a game with such a long history and predetermined lore.
